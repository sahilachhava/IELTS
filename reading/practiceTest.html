Setting up Exam Enviornment

Step 1: Get Book, Pen for reading exams.

Step 2: Divide page into half and number it from 1 to 40 (leave space maximum of 3 words)

Step 3: Sit in without distraction enviornment, and start the test.

Note 1: You should not pause it or go backwards if anything you didn't see / hear properly.

Note 2: You should only fill all the answers, if you missed some, no issue at all, be honest here.

Note 3: At the end of the test, you will see answers, check yourself and count your final score.

Note 4: Single character mistake or spelling mistakes are also considered as wrong.
